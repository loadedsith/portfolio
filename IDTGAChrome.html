It's Dangerous to Go Alone


Click to play!

In the span of 2 weeks a team of 3 people and I devloped this Zelda style 2d action / puzzeler with a twist: The only weapon is a magic beam drawn between the two players.

This was my first experience in Unity and it was an extremely impresive environment. We intend to consider this an early alpha and produce a real game from it, coming Fall 2013.

 

This game was created with the help of Jacob Money, Blair Kulhmen, and Jeremy Mack.

We desided to create a game which was dependent upon 2 people playing on the same keyboard. We were nestalgic for this type game play from our childhoods.
