Laser-cut garlic

This is another example of a number of skill sets. The end result is something beautiful, though it serves little use, it was highly educational in how to cut clear acrylic and how to write code for objects with Perlin noise seeds.

Enjoy the beautiful colors of my laser cut, acrylic, sliced, 3d model, of a head of garlic. It is illuminated by LEDs playing back an RGB sequence of Perlin Noise controlled by an Arduino
